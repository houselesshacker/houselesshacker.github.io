# Setup a Budget Field OSINT Device

### `Thank to mikeis075 for the feedback and advice.`

Conventional investigations are mostly passive research, but sometime you need to leave the office for physical inquisition, like collecting scans of VIN or plate, and monitoring live local incident. And you couldn't lug a laptop along the alley, so your phone becomes that purpose.

Field Phone should also follow the [burner practices](https://b3rn3d.herokuapp.com/blog/2014/01/22/burner-phone-best-practices/).

## Phone Setup

Whichever Android phone you prefer can be a field phone, however the recommendation is Pixel for its security, performance and ease of use. [You need a prepaid SIM service](https://prepaid-data-sim-card.fandom.com/wiki/Prepaid_SIM_with_data) for Google. Use an [ID Generator](https://hackmd.io/iw9juuFQSneDxTKDgv_Aew?view=) to generate burner metadata for the Google Account and anything else that link to the phone activity. Once you finished stock setup and updated your ROM version, you need to setup the basic apps.
